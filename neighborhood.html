Sugar House, Salt Lake City

Sugar house is a popular township of Salt Lake City located to the South East of downtown SLC. It is home to plenty of great restaurants and parks. It has a more suberban feel than downtown while only beening a few minutes away. The biggest draw though is the fast access to the neighboring mountains. within 30 minutes you can be deep into the mountains.
